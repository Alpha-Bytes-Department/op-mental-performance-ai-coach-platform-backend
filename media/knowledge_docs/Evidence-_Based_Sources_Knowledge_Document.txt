Evidence-Based Sources for OP Ai Coach to Pull Information From 

 

U.S. Department of Health & Human Services (HHS): Is one of the most definitive and foundational evidence-based sources for health information in the world. 

National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH): As the lead federal agency for research on mental disorders, the NIMH provides authoritative information on a wide range of mental health topics.  

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA): SAMHSA is a crucial resource for individuals seeking treatment for mental and/or substance use disorders. Their National Helpline (1-800-662-HELP) 

Mayo Clinic: This renowned medical center provides practical, research-backed tips for managing stress and improving overall wellbeing. 

Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC): The CDC provides extensive information on a wide range of health topics.  

World Health Organization (WHO): The WHO offers a global perspective on health and provides recommendations on lifestyle habits that prevent disease.  

National Institutes of Health (NIH): The NIH is a primary source for medical research and offers in-depth information on preventative care.  

American College of Lifestyle Medicine (ACLM): The ACLM is a professional medical association for physicians and other health professionals dedicated to the field of Lifestyle Medicine. 

Medical News Today: Medical News Today is a reputable source of health and medical information for the general public. 

American Psychological Association (APA): The APA is a leading scientific and professional organization representing psychology in the United States.
